📍 About St. Gallen Startup Tycoon

🎮 Game Overview

St. Gallen Startup Tycoon is a business simulation game where you build and manage your own startup in the beautiful Eastern Swiss city of St. Gallen.

🎯 Objective

Survive 12 months, build a successful company, and achieve the highest score on the leaderboard!

📊 Scoring

  • Balance: 1 point per CHF
  • Customers: 10 points each
  • Reputation: 1000 points per point
  • Product Quality: 1000 points per point

🎲 Game Mechanics

💼 Monthly Actions

  • Marketing: Gain customers and reputation
  • Development: Improve product quality
  • Hiring: Add employees for productivity
  • Research: Gain market insights and protection
  • Expansion: Major growth across Eastern Switzerland
  • Relaxation: Save money, potentially gain inspiration

🎭 Random Events

  • Swiss economic conditions
  • Local St. Gallen happenings
  • Business opportunities and crises
  • Interactive mini-games (Quiz, Dragons' Den)
  • Humorous Swiss-themed scenarios

💡 Tips for Success

💰 Financial Management

Watch your balance carefully. Going below -5,000 CHF means bankruptcy!

🎯 Balanced Growth

Don't neglect any metric. Balance customers, quality, and reputation.

🔬 Research Investment

Research protection helps reduce negative event impacts.

🎲 Risk vs. Reward

Some events offer high-risk, high-reward choices. Choose wisely!

⚠️ Bankruptcy & Loans

If your balance goes negative, you can take emergency loans to survive:

  • Emergency Loan: 5,000 CHF immediate cash
  • Repayment: 2,000 CHF per month for 3 months
  • High Interest: Pay back 6,000 CHF total
  • Last Resort: Better than bankruptcy!

If you can't repay loans and reach -5,000 CHF, it's game over!
