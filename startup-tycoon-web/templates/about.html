📍 About St. Gallen Startup Tycoon

🎮 Game Overview

St. Gallen Startup Tycoon is a business simulation game where you build and manage your own startup in the beautiful Eastern Swiss city of St. Gallen.

🎯 Objective

Survive 12 months, build a successful company, and achieve the highest score on the leaderboard!

📊 Scoring

  • Balance: 1 point per CHF
  • Customers: 10 points each
  • Reputation: 1000 points per point
  • Product Quality: 1000 points per point

🧩 Monthly Actions and Exact Effects

📣 HSG Campus Marketing (1,500 CHF)

  • +15 to +35 customers
  • +0.1 to +0.3 reputation
  • Economic Boom event: next 2 Marketing actions have double effect.

🛠️ Swiss Quality Development (2,000 CHF)

  • +0.3 to +0.5 product quality
  • +5 to +15 customers

👥 Recruit Talent (3,000 CHF)

  • +1 employee
  • +8 to +20 customers (productivity boost)
  • +0.1 to +0.2 product quality
  • Monthly costs +2,000 CHF for each employee

🔬 Market Research (1,000 CHF)

  • +1 research protection
  • +5 to +12 customers
  • Helps mitigate certain negative events like legal fines and bureaucracy delays.

🏢 Regional Expansion (4,000 CHF)

  • +25 to +45 customers
  • +1 employee (monthly costs +2,000 CHF)
  • +0.2 to +0.4 reputation
  • +0.2 to +0.4 product quality

🏖️ Chill at Drei Weihern (free)

  • 30% chance for inspiration: +3 to +8 customers
  • Otherwise no direct effect

💵 How Profit and Loss Are Calculated

📈 Monthly Revenue

Each month, if there is no special event blocking revenue:

      
The monthly revenue is calculated by the amout of customer × reputation × base revenue (randomly between 5 and 8)

      
Example: 200 customers, quality 2.0, reputation 1.5 gives roughly 200 × 2.0 × 1.5 × ~6.5 ≈ 3,900 CHF.

💸 Monthly Operating Costs

      
The monthly costs is calculated by 500 + the amout of employees × 2000

      
Events can temporarily increase or reduce costs. Examples: interest rate rise +500 CHF per month, tax reduction −500 CHF per month, startup dog +150 to +250 CHF per month.

🎭 Random Events

  • Economy and local St. Gallen happenings can add or remove customers and balance.
  • Some events adjust monthly costs or temporarily block revenue for one month.
  • Interactive events (Quiz, Trade Fair, Dragons Den) may ask for choices with risk and reward.
  • Research protection reduces the impact of several negative events.

⚠️ Bankruptcy and Loans

  • Emergency Loan: 5,000 CHF immediate cash
  • Repayment: 2,000 CHF per month for 3 months
  • Total payback: 6,000 CHF
  • Game over: Balance below −5,000 CHF

💡 Tips for Success

💰 Financial Management

Watch your balance carefully. Going below -5,000 CHF means bankruptcy.

🎯 Balanced Growth

Balance customers, quality, and reputation for sustainable revenue.

🔬 Research Investment

Research protection helps reduce negative event impacts.

🎲 Risk vs. Reward

Some events offer high risk and high reward. Choose wisely.
